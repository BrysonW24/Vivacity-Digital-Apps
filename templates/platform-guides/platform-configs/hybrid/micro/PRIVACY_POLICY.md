# Privacy Policy for Journal App

**Effective Date:** October 30, 2025

## Information We Collect

Journal App is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or journal entries.

### What We Don't Collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Journal entries, notes, or any user-generated content
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Any other data

## Data Storage

All journal entries are stored locally on your device only. Your data never leaves your device and is not backed up to any cloud service or external server.

### Permissions Used:
- **Storage**: To export/share journal entries (optional)
- **Notifications**: For reminder features (optional)
- **Internet**: Only for potential future features like cloud sync (currently not implemented)

## Data Security

- Your data remains on your device
- No internet connection required for core functionality
- No data collection or tracking

## Third Parties

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we decide to modify our privacy practices, we will update this policy and notify users through app updates.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us at: privacy@vivacitydigital.com
